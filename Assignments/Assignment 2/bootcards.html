Legends of Cricket

Virat Kohli

Top Order Batsman

Known as "King Kohli," he is regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time, holding numerous records in ODI and Test cricket for India.

MS Dhoni

Wicketkeeper Batsman

"Captain Cool" led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ODI World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy. Famous for his finishing skills.

Sachin Tendulkar

Legendary Batsman

Known as the "God of Cricket," Sachin has the most runs in international cricket history and is the only player to score one hundred international centuries.
